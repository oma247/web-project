What is Prenatal Massage?

Prenatal massage is a therapeutic bodywork that focuses on the special needs of the mother-to-be. It helps reduce stress, decrease swelling in the arms and legs, and relieve muscle and joint pain.It's a gentle and safe practice performed by trained therapists using special positioning and techniques.

Benefits:

  • Reduces anxiety and stress
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Relieves lower back pain
  • Improves circulation and lymphatic flow
  • Supports emotional wellness
