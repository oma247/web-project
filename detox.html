What is Detoxification?

Detoxification is the process of eliminating toxins from the body to improve health and wellness. This lesson covers safe and natural ways to support your organs, especially the liver, kidneys, and colon.

Benefits

  • Boosts energy and mental clarity
  • Improves digestion and nutrient absorption
  • Supports weight management
  • Enhances skin clarity and immune function

Natural Detox Methods

  • Herbal teas (e.g., dandelion, nettle, burdock)
  • Hydration with lemon water
  • High-fiber foods
  • Intermittent fasting
  • Dry brushing and light exercise
