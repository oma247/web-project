Making Herbal Teas & Tonics

Learn how to brew natural remedies for wellness

Overview

Herbal teas and tonics are a simple, powerful way to harness the benefits of medicinal herbs. Whether for immunity, digestion, sleep, or energy, there’s a tea blend for nearly every need.

Common Herbs Used

  • Ginger – for digestion and anti-inflammation
  • Peppermint – relieves nausea and refreshes
  • Chamomile – calming and sleep-inducing
  • Hibiscus – supports heart health
  • Turmeric – antioxidant and anti-inflammatory

Preparation Steps

  1. Choose dried or fresh herbs based on the desired benefit.
  2. Boil 1-2 cups of water.
  3. Add 1 teaspoon of dried herbs or 1 tablespoon of fresh herbs.
  4. Steep for 5–10 minutes with a lid on.
  5. Strain and enjoy warm. Add honey or lemon if desired.
Tip: Tonics are usually taken over time. Brew larger quantities and refrigerate for daily use.

Benefits

  • Natural healing with fewer side effects
  • Supports body systems gently and effectively
  • Customizable blends to fit personal needs
  • Encourages hydration and wellness rituals
