Herbal Remedies for Common Ailments

Learn natural remedies you can use for everyday health issues.

1. Headaches

Peppermint tea and Lavender oil can help relieve tension headaches. Peppermint improves blood flow while lavender calms the nervous system.

2. Indigestion

Ginger and Chamomile are great for indigestion. Ginger reduces bloating and nausea, while chamomile relaxes the stomach muscles.

3. Cough & Cold

Honey & Lemon tea soothes sore throats, while Thyme tea helps loosen mucus.

4. Insomnia

Valerian root and Chamomile tea are well-known natural sleep aids.

5. Stress & Anxiety

Ashwagandha and Passionflower tea can help reduce anxiety and improve relaxation.
