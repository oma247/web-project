Introduction to Natural Healing

What is Herbal Medicine?

Herbal medicine is the use of plants to treat and prevent illnesses. It has been used for centuries in various cultures worldwide.

Common Herbs and Uses

  • Ginger: Aids digestion and reduces nausea.
  • Turmeric: Anti-inflammatory and antioxidant.
  • Peppermint: Helps with indigestion and headaches.

Safety Tips

Always consult with a healthcare professional before starting any herbal treatments. Some herbs may interact with medications or cause allergic reactions.
