Workshops & Herbal Learning Classes

At Bellwether Naturecure Clinic, we believe that healing knowledge should be shared. Our Herbal Workshops and Learning Classes are designed to equip individuals with practical, foundational knowledge in alternative medicine, plant-based healing, and holistic wellness practices.

What You’ll Learn

  • Introduction to Herbalism: history, safety, and usage
  • How to identify, prepare, and store medicinal herbs
  • Creating herbal teas, tinctures, oils, and poultices
  • Understanding the body's natural healing systems
  • Nutrition and lifestyle support through herbs
  • Managing stress, immunity, and common ailments naturally

Programs Offered

  • Beginner Herbal Course (2 weeks)
  • Intermediate Plant Medicine (1 month)
  • Specialty Classes — focused on fertility, immunity, and detox
  • Hands-on Workshops — making your own herbal remedies

Who Can Enroll?

Whether you're a curious beginner, a wellness enthusiast, or a professional looking to expand your natural healing knowledge — our courses are open to all. No prior medical or herbal training is required.

What You’ll Get

  • Expert-led live or recorded classes
  • PDF learning materials and herbal recipes
  • Hands-on assignments and remedy-making
  • Certificate of Completion
  • Ongoing access to learning community

Delivery Format

Our courses are available both online and in-person (at our clinic location). Students can choose a flexible schedule and learn at their own pace.
