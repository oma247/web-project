Lesson: Basics of Herbal Preparation

Learn how to prepare herbal remedies through infusion, decoction, tincture, salves, and poultices.

  • Infusions: Steeping herbs in hot water like tea.
  • Decoctions: Boiling tougher parts like roots.
  • Tinctures: Using alcohol or vinegar as extract base.
  • Salves: Combining herbs with wax or oil for topical use.
