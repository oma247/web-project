Making Herbal Oils & Balms

What You’ll Learn

This course teaches how to infuse herbs into oils and turn them into healing balms. You’ll explore carrier oils, herb selection, and methods like solar and heat infusion. Perfect for skincare, massage, and minor wounds.

Tools You’ll Need

  • Clean glass jars
  • Carrier oils (olive, coconut, almond)
  • Herbs (lavender, calendula, comfrey, etc.)
  • Beeswax (for balm making)
  • Double boiler or slow cooker

Sample Lesson Highlights

  • How to choose the right herbs and oils
  • Solar vs. heat infusion
  • Turning oil into balm
  • Storage and shelf life tips
