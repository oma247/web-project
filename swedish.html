Swedish Massage

What is Swedish Massage?

Swedish massage is a gentle, full-body therapeutic massage designed to relax the body and calm the mind. Using long, gliding strokes, kneading, and circular movements, this technique improves circulation and eases muscle tension.

Benefits

  • Relieves stress and promotes relaxation
  • Improves blood circulation
  • Reduces muscle tension and soreness
  • Boosts overall well-being and sleep quality
